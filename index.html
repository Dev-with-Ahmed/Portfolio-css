About Me

Personal Journey

My passion for creativity and technology started early — around the age of 7 or 8 — when I first discovered Photoshop. I used to edit my own photos, swapping my face with superheroes, and it felt like magic. That small spark grew into a deep interest in digital skills, from designing thumbnails to building websites and even creating games.

What keeps me going is a simple desire: to learn, grow, and become someone I can be proud of — someone my younger self would look up to. It’s not always easy; building a coding habit and staying focused while distractions like games, Netflix, and just chilling are always around is a real struggle. But I try — sometimes I win, sometimes I fall short — and that’s okay.

This journey isn’t just about skills; it’s about not living with regrets. I want to know I gave it my best shot. Every line of code, every project, every failure and small win — it’s all part of becoming the kind of person I once dreamed of becoming.
